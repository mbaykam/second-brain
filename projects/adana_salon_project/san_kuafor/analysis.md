## 4. Recommended AI Solutions

### Solution #1: WhatsApp AI Chatbot for 24/7 Appointment Booking
**What it does:**
- Handles appointment requests via WhatsApp (Turkey's #1 messaging app)
- Checks real-time availability against salon calendar
- Confirms bookings instantly with SMS/WhatsApp confirmation
- Sends automated reminders (24h, 2h before)
- Handles cancellations/rescheduling

**Tech Stack:**
- WhatsApp Business API
- Integration with existing salon calendar (or Salon Randevu/Arvengo)
- Turkish NLP for natural conversation

**Expected Impact:**
- **Time Saved:** 15-20 hours/week staff time
- **Revenue Impact:** 15-20% reduction in no-shows = ~30.000-50.000 TL/month at current volume
- **Customer Satisfaction:** 24/7 instant response vs. waiting for business hours

### Solution #2: AI-Powered Instagram DM Automation
**What it does:**
- Auto-responds to common DM inquiries ("Randevu alabilir miyim?", "Fiyat nedir?")
- Qualifies leads (service type, preferred date, contact info)
- Escalates complex requests to human staff
- Sends price list and service catalog automatically

**Tech Stack:**
- Instagram Graph API
- AI response system with Turkish language support
- Handoff protocol to human agents

**Expected Impact:**
- **Time Saved:** 10-15 hours/week
- **Response Time:** From hours to seconds
- **Lead Capture:** 100% of DMs responded to vs. current partial coverage

### Solution #3: Smart CRM with Customer Memory
**What it does:**
- Tracks customer preferences (tesettür section, preferred stylist, past services)
- Automated follow-ups ("3 months since your last keratin treatment")
- Birthday/anniversary offers
- Personalized service recommendations

**Tech Stack:**
- Cloud-based CRM (HubSpot/Salon-specific)
- Integration with booking system
- Automated SMS/WhatsApp campaigns

**Expected Impact:**
- **Retention:** 20-30% increase in repeat customers

- **Staff Efficiency:** Instant access to customer history
